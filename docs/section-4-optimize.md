# 4. Analyzing and Optimizing Technical and Business Processes

## 4.1 Analyzing and Defining Technical Processes

- **SDLC**: CI/CD pipelines using Cloud Build.
- **Testing**: Automated testing and canary deployments.
- **Disaster Recovery**: RTO/RPO planning, backup strategies.

## 4.2 Analyzing and Defining Business Processes

- **Stakeholder Management**: Aligning technical decisions with business goals.
- **Cost Management**: Using Budgets, Alerts, and Billing Export to BigQuery.
- **Crisis Management**: Incident response planning.

## 4.3 Developing Procedures to Ensure Reliability of Operations in Production

- **Chaos Engineering**: Testing system resilience.
- **Post-mortems**: Learning from incidents (blameless culture).

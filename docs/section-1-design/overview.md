# Overview

This section covers the foundational skills required to design a robust cloud architecture that aligns with both business and technical requirements.

## What You'll Learn

- How to translate business requirements into technical solutions
- Cost optimization strategies and pricing models
- Integration patterns for hybrid and multi-cloud environments
- Data migration strategies and tools
- Network, storage, and compute resource design
- Migration planning and execution strategies

## Exam Weight

This section represents approximately **24%** of the exam questions.

## Key Topics

1. **Business Requirements** - Understanding use cases, cost optimization, and integration needs
2. **Technical Requirements** - High availability, scalability, and reliability
3. **Resource Design** - Choosing the right compute, storage, and network services
4. **Migration Planning** - Strategies and tools for moving to the cloud
5. **Future Improvements** - Planning for evolution and growth

:::tip Study Approach
Focus on understanding **why** you would choose one service over another in specific scenarios. The exam tests your decision-making ability, not just memorization.
:::

# TerramEarth

## Company Overview
TerramEarth manufactures heavy equipment for the mining and agricultural industries. They currently have over 500 dealers and service centers in 100 countries.

## Solution Concept
TerramEarth wants to collect data from their connected vehicles to predict maintenance needs and improve vehicle design. They also want to provide a portal for dealers to access this data.

## Existing Technical Environment
- **Data Collection**: FTP servers.
- **Data Center**: On-premises.
- **Connectivity**: MPLS.

## Business Requirements
- **Uptime**: Minimize vehicle downtime.
- **Innovation**: Create new revenue streams from data.
- **Partners**: Share data securely with dealers.

## Technical Requirements
- **IoT**: Securely ingest data from millions of devices.
- **API**: Expose data via APIs.
- **Storage**: Store petabytes of historical data.
